# Supporting Cast of Solterra Bay

## Overview

This document lists the recurring supporting characters of Solterra Bay, organized by their primary location. The core cast (Cornelius, Imelda, and Zeph) have their own dedicated document.

Many characters appear in multiple locations, but each is listed where they're most likely to be found.

### Species Distribution Check

To reflect the town's demographics (40% human, 35% mythological kin, 12% elf, 10% orc, 3% dwarf), the supporting cast includes:

| Species | Characters | Notes |
|---------|------------|-------|
| Humans | ~15 | Various heritages |
| Mythological Kin | ~14 | Sirens, gorgons, satyr-kin, nymph-touched, etc. |
| Elves | ~3 | Cyprian and Etruscan |
| Orcs | ~4 | Phoenic heritage |
| Dwarves | ~1 | Opportunity for development |

Core cast (Cornelius, Imelda, Zeph) tracked separately.

## The Library

### Library Regulars

**Yiayia Despina Kostopoulos** — The Elder
- **Species:** Human (Hellenic heritage)
- **Age:** 83
- **Appearance:** Small, weathered, white hair in a bun, always in black (widowed decades ago), sharp dark eyes that miss nothing
- **Role:** Living memory of Solterra Bay; was a child when Cornelius arrived
- **Location:** Has claimed the sunny reading corner as her territory
- **Personality:** Seemingly sweet, actually sharp; cryptic hints disguised as rambling; brings tyropita weekly
- **Key trait:** Cornelius reserves her chair every morning and pretends this annoys him
- **Knows:** Town history, old scandals, who's related to whom, where the bodies are buried (metaphorically; possibly literally)

**Brother Demos** — The Eager Borrower
- **Species:** Half-elf (Cyprian)
- **Age:** 28 (young for a half-elf)
- **Appearance:** Warm brown skin, eager expression, always slightly disheveled, ink-stained fingers
- **Role:** Junior healer at the Temple of Althaia; constantly requesting obscure medical texts
- **Location:** Splits time between temple and library
- **Personality:** Enthusiastic about ailments he'll never encounter, endearingly optimistic
- **Key trait:** Returns books on time (Cornelius approves) but asks too many questions (Cornelius does not)

**Dimitra Kallistos** — The Researcher
- **Species:** Gorgon
- **Age:** 34
- **Appearance:** Olive skin with subtle scaling on cheekbones, snake-hair kept short and tidy (the snakes are calm, well-behaved), golden eyes with slit pupils, professional demeanor
- **Role:** Heir to House Kallistos tile family; uses library for historical pattern research
- **Location:** Library's history and art sections; also the Kallistos compound
- **Personality:** Technically brilliant, quietly innovative, caught between tradition and evolution
- **Key trait:** Her research visits may be partly an excuse to be away from her grandfather's oversight
- **Potential arc:** Forbidden-ish romance with Markos Veneti; stepping into leadership

## Cassius's Bakery

**Cassius Nerva** — The Baker
- **Species:** Satyr-kin
- **Age:** 48
- **Appearance:** Human upper body with goat legs, small curved horns almost hidden in thick curly grey-brown hair, neat beard, flour perpetually dusting his forearms, warm brown eyes, cloven hooves
- **Role:** Fourth-generation baker, keeper of the great oven, information hub
- **Personality:** Warm, steady, patient; listens more than he speaks; never gossips but remembers everything
- **Key trait:** Knows everyone's business because they tell him while waiting for their bread
- **Potential arc:** Slow-burn romance with Imelda
- **Note:** Changed from human to satyr-kin; fits the Mediterranean setting and goat-herding culture better

**Elpida Nerva** — The Niece
- **Species:** Satyr-kin
- **Age:** 22
- **Appearance:** Brown skin, dark curly hair usually tied back, small horn nubs, goat legs, quick smile, always moving
- **Role:** Cassius's niece and apprentice; will inherit the bakery someday
- **Personality:** Energetic, friendly, gossips freely (unlike her uncle), ambitious
- **Key trait:** Wants to expand the bakery; Cassius thinks it's fine as it is
- **Useful for:** Current gossip, young adult perspective, reaching people Cassius wouldn't

### Bakery Regulars

The bakery sees most of the town pass through. Key regulars include:

**Old Petros** — The Early Riser
- **Species:** Human (Hellenic)
- **Age:** 70s
- **Role:** First customer every morning; retired fisherman
- **Key trait:** Delivers news from the harbor; knows tide schedules better than his own name

**The Kallistos Servant**
- **Species:** Naiad-touched
- **Age:** 40s
- **Role:** Picks up bread for the Kallistos household; quietly reports back to both families
- **Key trait:** Hears everything, appears to hear nothing

## The Oar & Anchor Tavern

**Despina Kraikos** — The Landlady
- **Species:** Orc (Phoenic heritage)
- **Age:** 55
- **Appearance:** Deep green skin weathered by years of sea air, tusks filed to practical points, grey-streaked black hair in a practical braid, sharp eyes, strong hands
- **Role:** Runs the tavern's front of house, handles money, keeps order
- **Personality:** Sharp, quick, misses nothing, unflappable; has seen everything twice
- **Key trait:** Knows when to remember things and when to forget them
- **Note:** Changed from human to orc; makes the tavern feel more integrated

**Yannis Kraikos** — The Cook
- **Species:** Human (Hellenic heritage)
- **Age:** 58
- **Appearance:** Big, calm, scarred hands from decades of kitchen work, receding grey hair, patient expression
- **Role:** Runs the kitchen, handles drunks when necessary, married to Despina
- **Personality:** Quiet, steady, rarely raises his voice (doesn't need to)
- **Key trait:** His kakavia recipe is a closely guarded secret

**Captain Mago Hanno** — The Regular
- **Species:** Orc (Phoenic heritage)
- **Age:** 52
- **Appearance:** Grey-brown skin, prominent tusks (unpolished, a point of pride), salt-and-pepper hair in sailor's knots, weathered face, missing part of one ear
- **Role:** Runs the largest fishing boat in the harbor; unofficial leader of the fishing fleet
- **Location:** His unofficial reserved spot on the tavern's porch
- **Personality:** Gruff exterior; secretly reads romance novels (borrows them under fake names)
- **Key trait:** His crew is loyal beyond reason; he's earned it
- **Useful for:** Harbor information, smuggling mysteries, secret romantic

### Mago's Crew (Tavern Regulars)

**Thessaly** — The Singer
- **Species:** Siren
- **Age:** 35
- **Appearance:** Iridescent skin, hair the color of deep water, solid sea-green eyes without visible whites, webbed fingers usually hidden in pockets
- **Role:** Mago's first mate; best navigator on the crew
- **Personality:** Quiet, competent, tired of the "dangerous siren" jokes
- **Key trait:** Her singing keeps the crew's spirits up on long nights; it's just beautiful, not magical compulsion

**Brontes "One-Eye"** — The Strong Arm
- **Species:** Cyclops descendant
- **Age:** 45
- **Appearance:** Seven feet tall, massive build, one eye significantly larger than the other (not actually one-eyed, but the nickname stuck), ruddy skin
- **Role:** Handles heavy lifting on Mago's boat and bouncer duties at the tavern
- **Personality:** Gentle until he isn't; fiercely protective of crewmates
- **Key trait:** Surprisingly good at delicate tasks; repairs nets with unexpected precision

**Kosta Pelagios** — The Young One
- **Species:** Nereid-touched
- **Age:** 19
- **Appearance:** Iridescent hints to olive skin, hair with blue-black sheen, can hold his breath for ten minutes
- **Role:** Newest member of Mago's crew; excellent diver
- **Personality:** Eager to prove himself, sometimes reckless, good heart
- **Key trait:** Might have a crush on Zeph (potential Book 3-4 subplot)

### Other Tavern Regulars

**Alexandros Doukas** — The Charmer
- **Species:** Human (Hellenic heritage)
- **Age:** 58
- **Appearance:** Silver-touched dark hair, easy smile, clothes slightly too fine for the tavern, hands that have never done manual labor
- **Role:** The Doukas tile family's salesman; buys drinks, makes deals, knows everyone
- **Personality:** Charming, persuasive, makes promises the workshop can't always keep
- **Key trait:** Never met a stranger; information flows to him naturally
- **Location:** Tavern most evenings, tile market stall on market days

## The Bathhouse

**Old Nikos** — The Keeper
- **Species:** Human (Hellenic heritage)
- **Age:** 70
- **Appearance:** Lean, weathered, bald, surprisingly strong for his age, calm eyes that have seen everyone naked
- **Role:** Runs the bathhouse; assigns newcomers to pools; keeper of secrets
- **Personality:** Discreet as a stone; the closest thing to a therapist Solterra Bay has
- **Key trait:** Knows everyone's bodies—scars, ailments, secrets—and says nothing
- **Useful for:** Noticing when something is wrong with someone; neutral ground conversations

**Phoebe** — The Attendant
- **Species:** Naiad-touched
- **Age:** 25
- **Appearance:** Faint blue-green tinge to pale skin, wet-looking dark hair, cool to the touch, moves like water
- **Role:** Senior bathhouse attendant; manages towels, oils, heating
- **Personality:** Calm, efficient, genuinely enjoys the work (water affinity)
- **Key trait:** Sees everything, knows everyone's habits, occasionally shares information with the right people

**Damon** — The Furnace Keeper
- **Species:** Chimera-blooded (fire lizard traits)
- **Age:** 30
- **Appearance:** Human mostly, but with subtle scaling on hands and forearms, eyes with vertical pupils, runs unusually warm
- **Role:** Tends the bathhouse furnace; keeps the pools at proper temperatures
- **Personality:** Quiet, prefers the heat of the furnace room to conversation
- **Key trait:** His chimera heritage makes him perfect for the job; he's comfortable where others would swelter

### Bathhouse Regulars

**Master Stavros Kallistos** — The Patriarch
- **Species:** Gorgon
- **Age:** 71
- **Appearance:** Distinguished, grey-scaled skin, snake-hair silver with age (the snakes are slower now, but still watchful), formal even when undressed
- **Role:** Head of House Kallistos; takes the Hot Pool most afternoons
- **Location:** Bathhouse (Hot Pool), Kallistos compound
- **Personality:** Fierce guardian of tradition, grudgingly respects quality regardless of source
- **Key trait:** Still working daily; refuses to hand over control to Dimitra

**Master Kostas Veneti** — The Merchant
- **Species:** Human (mixed heritage)
- **Age:** 55
- **Appearance:** Prosperous-looking even naked, olive skin, calculating eyes, trader's smile
- **Role:** Head of House Veneti tiles; takes the Merchant's Pool
- **Personality:** Businessman first, craftsman second; respects artistry but loves contracts
- **Key trait:** Often conducts business in the bathhouse; neutral ground for negotiations

## The Docks

**Harbor Master Theron Gavros** — The Official
- **Species:** Human (Hellenic heritage)
- **Age:** 50
- **Appearance:** Former sailor, weathered face, missing two fingers on left hand (old injury, healed well), practical clothes, ink-stained ledger always nearby
- **Role:** Registers ships, collects fees, settles disputes, keeps records
- **Personality:** Efficient, fair, mildly corrupt (small bribes for small favors, never anything serious)
- **Key trait:** Knows every ship that's passed through in twenty years
- **Useful for:** Ship manifests, arrival/departure times, who's been where

**Nephele** — The Fish Merchant
- **Species:** Nereid-touched
- **Age:** 45
- **Appearance:** Stocky, strong, grey-green hair always wet, iridescent skin, booming voice
- **Role:** Runs the morning fish auction; first point of sale for the fishing fleet
- **Personality:** Loud, fair, takes no nonsense, knows fish quality by smell alone
- **Key trait:** The fish market is her domain; she decides who gets the best catch

**Silas the Shipwright**
- **Species:** Oread-touched (mountain)
- **Age:** 60
- **Appearance:** Grey-toned skin, heavier than he looks, stone-dust perpetually in his white hair, steady hands
- **Role:** Runs the shipwright's yard; builds and repairs boats
- **Personality:** Patient, meticulous, speaks slowly and means every word
- **Key trait:** An oread-touched who chose the sea—unusual; has reasons he doesn't discuss
- **Useful for:** Modifications to boats, knowing what vessels have been worked on

**Korinna** — The Dock Worker
- **Species:** Chimera-blooded (leonine traits)
- **Age:** 28
- **Appearance:** Tawny skin, cat-like eyes, mane of tawny hair, unusually strong, moves with feline grace
- **Role:** Loading, unloading, heavy labor; informal leader of the dock workers
- **Personality:** Direct, loyal to her crew, doesn't tolerate mistreatment of workers
- **Key trait:** Notices everything that moves through the docks; knows which crates are heavier than they should be

**Captain Yiannis Veneti** — The Trader
- **Species:** Human (mixed heritage)
- **Age:** 50
- **Appearance:** Salt-weathered skin, sailor's build, family resemblance to brother Kostas but rougher
- **Role:** Runs the Veneti trading ship; carries tiles out, brings supplies in
- **Personality:** Technically a sea captain, practically a family ambassador
- **Key trait:** Gossip from other ports; knows prices and rumors from up and down the coast
- **Location:** Docks when in port; at sea frequently

## Temple of Althaia

**Sister Photine** — Head Healer
- **Species:** Human (Hellenic heritage)
- **Age:** 52
- **Appearance:** Practical, kind face, grey-streaked brown hair pinned back, healer's apron always faintly stained with herbs, capable hands
- **Role:** Head healer at the Temple of Althaia; runs the herb garden
- **Personality:** Practical, unflappable, knows everyone's medical history
- **Key trait:** Patients talk while being treated; she knows secrets people don't realize they've shared
- **Relationship with library:** Returns books on time; Cornelius respects her

**Brother Demos** — Junior Healer
- See Library section; splits time between both locations

**Acolyte Melaina** — The Novice
- **Species:** Dryad-touched
- **Age:** 17
- **Appearance:** Bark-textured patches on hands and forearms, hair with greenish tint that shifts with seasons, smells faintly of olive wood
- **Role:** Newest acolyte; training in healing and herb cultivation
- **Personality:** Shy, gifted with plants, struggles with the human-interaction parts of healing
- **Key trait:** The herb garden flourishes under her care; she's better with plants than people

### Temple Regulars

The temple sees people when they're vulnerable. Regular visitors include the chronically ill, the elderly managing ongoing conditions, and those seeking peace.

**Old Yorgos** — The Arthritic Fisherman
- **Species:** Human
- **Age:** 75
- **Role:** Comes weekly for joint treatment; tells the same stories every time
- **Key trait:** Knows tides and weather from sixty years of fishing

## The Washhouse

**Stavroula** — The Washhouse Manager
- **Species:** Naiad-touched
- **Age:** 55
- **Appearance:** Blue-tinged veins visible under fair skin, grey hair always damp, calm expression, water seems to move easier around her
- **Role:** Unofficial manager of the washhouse; maintains the water flow and settles disputes
- **Personality:** Patient, observant, arbitrates disagreements with quiet authority
- **Key trait:** Has been washing clothes at this spot for forty years; knows everyone's laundry (and therefore everyone's secrets)

**Rhea** — The Gossip
- **Species:** Satyr-kin
- **Age:** 40
- **Appearance:** Curly brown hair, small horns, goat legs, animated expressions, always talking
- **Role:** Does laundry for three households; professional gossip
- **Personality:** Friendly, curious, incapable of keeping quiet
- **Key trait:** If you want a rumor spread, tell Rhea; if you want to find a rumor's source, ask Rhea
- **Useful for:** Information distribution, tracing gossip chains

**Markos Veneti** — The Unexpected Regular
- **Species:** Human (mixed heritage)
- **Age:** 28
- **Appearance:** Handsome in a soft way, merchant's son clothes that he tries to dress down, ink-stained fingers (does his own accounts)
- **Role:** Heir to House Veneti; does his own laundry (unusual for his class) as excuse to be in places his father wouldn't expect
- **Personality:** Genuinely interested in craft over commerce; uncomfortable with his expected role
- **Key trait:** His presence in working-class spaces is noticed and discussed
- **Potential arc:** Forbidden-ish romance with Dimitra Kallistos; may be a library ally

## The Main Square

**Mayor Tanit Barca** — The Official
- **Species:** Orc (Phoenic heritage)
- **Age:** 48
- **Appearance:** Deep green skin, tusks capped with brass (traditional), black hair in official braids, clothes practical but mayoral, permanent expression of someone with too much to do
- **Role:** Mayor of Solterra Bay; makes official announcements from the town hall loggia
- **Personality:** Pragmatic, overworked, genuinely cares about the town but has limited resources
- **Key trait:** Not hostile to the library but needs convincing it's worth funding
- **Useful for:** Official permissions, town politics, bureaucratic obstacles or assistance

**Town Crier Philo** — The Announcer
- **Species:** Satyr-kin
- **Age:** 35
- **Appearance:** Impressive set of curved horns, powerful lungs, theatrical manner, goat legs built for standing all day
- **Role:** Makes public announcements, reads proclamations, spreads official news
- **Personality:** Dramatic, loves his job, genuinely believes in civic duty
- **Key trait:** His voice carries across the entire square; unofficial arbiter of what's "real" news

### Market Day Regulars

**Master Eleni Doukas** — The Artist
- **Species:** Human (Hellenic heritage)  
- **Age:** 63
- **Appearance:** Paint-stained fingers, distracted expression, beautiful chaos of grey-streaked dark hair, clothes that were expensive before she started using them as paint rags
- **Role:** Head of House Doukas tiles; creative genius, terrible businesswoman
- **Personality:** Brilliant, easily distracted, keeps experimenting instead of producing inventory
- **Key trait:** Invented more glaze colors than anyone in three generations; can't deliver orders on time
- **Location:** Rarely at the market stall; Alexandros handles that

**Thalia Veneti** — The Craftswoman
- **Species:** Elf (Cyprian)
- **Age:** 140 (appears 50ish)
- **Appearance:** Bronze skin, copper-beaded braids, elegant even in work clothes, hands that show real craft despite her husband's business focus
- **Role:** Kostas Veneti's wife; the actual master ceramicist who ensures Veneti quality
- **Personality:** Quietly furious that her husband gets credit for "Veneti quality"; teaches apprentices
- **Key trait:** Actually likes Eleni Doukas; they bond over being craftswomen in family businesses
- **Note:** Changed from human to Cyprian elf; adds elven representation and explains her longevity in the craft

**Goat Vendor Pelagia**
- **Species:** Satyr-kin
- **Age:** 60
- **Appearance:** Grey-furred legs, impressive horns, weathered face, knows every goat in the region by name
- **Role:** Sells goat cheese, milk, and occasionally goats at market
- **Personality:** Opinionated about goats, dismissive about everything else
- **Key trait:** Her family supplies the town's goat products; she's known everyone since they were buying milk for their babies

## The Widow's Perch

The Widow's Perch doesn't have staff or regulars in the traditional sense. It's a place people go to be alone. But certain figures are occasionally seen there:

**The Grieving Widow (Unnamed)**
- **Species:** Varies
- **Role:** Whoever has most recently lost someone to the sea
- **Key trait:** The perch serves its original purpose; there's usually someone there at sunset

**Cornelius**
- Visits occasionally
- Claims it's for the updrafts (good for flying)
- Actually goes when he needs to think
- Will leave if others arrive; respects the space's purpose

**Imelda**
- Goes when she needs perspective
- Where she went after her mother died (decades ago)
- Might be found there during emotional turning points in the series

### Other Businesses

**Lyra Theron** — The Schoolteacher
- **Species:** Sphinx-kin
- **Age:** 45
- **Appearance:** Human upper body, lioness lower body (compact, not oversized), small wings usually folded, cat-like golden eyes, patient expression
- **Role:** Runs the local school; assigns students to internships (including Zeph's library placement)
- **Personality:** Genuinely loves teaching; hopes the library will inspire students
- **Key trait:** Uses riddles in her lessons; the children either love or dread this
- **Note:** Changed from human to sphinx-kin; adds rare species representation and fits the scholarly role

**Vasilios** — The Wine Shop Empousa
- **Species:** Empousa
- **Age:** Unknown (empousai don't share)
- **Appearance:** Strikingly handsome in a slightly-too-perfect way, one leg hidden under long robes, eyes that sometimes catch light oddly
- **Role:** Runs the best wine shop in town; "reformed" (feeds ethically)
- **Personality:** Charming, knowledgeable about wine, carefully maintains respectability
- **Key trait:** Still makes some residents nervous; useful for mysteries as red herring or unexpected ally
- **Note:** Given a name; changed to male to vary representation

**Medusa Petraki** — The Stonecutter
- **Species:** Gorgon
- **Age:** 50
- **Appearance:** Muscular, stone-dust perpetually on her clothes, snake-hair kept practically short, impressive focused stare
- **Role:** Master stonecutter; runs the masonry business with her family
- **Personality:** Professional, takes pride in work, dry humor
- **Key trait:** Yes, her name is Medusa; she's heard all the jokes; her stare really is uncomfortable

**Tarchon Velthuri** — The Antiquarian
- **Species:** Elf (Etruscan)
- **Age:** 200+
- **Appearance:** Pale grey-green skin, iron-grey hair, unsettling pale gold eyes, clothes from three different decades, dust on everything
- **Role:** Runs antique shop full of mysterious objects; knows provenance of everything
- **Personality:** Secretive, shares information reluctantly, possibly connected to artifact smuggling
- **Key trait:** Cornelius disapproves of him; they have excellent antagonistic chemistry
- **Useful for:** Mysterious objects, historical information, morally grey assistance

**Dr. Kiya Amenhet** — The Physician
- **Species:** Sphinx-kin
- **Age:** 55
- **Appearance:** Lioness lower body with spotted fur (unusual pattern), human torso with Aegyptian features, small wings, spectacles perched on her nose
- **Role:** Town physician (works alongside Temple of Althaia for serious cases)
- **Personality:** Scientific-minded, respects old knowledge, collects ancient medical texts
- **Key trait:** Has an extensive collection of healing texts she'd love to donate to the library
- **Note:** Changed from human to sphinx-kin; connects the scholarly sphinx tradition to medicine
