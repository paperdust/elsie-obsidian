# Story Bible: Book 2 - "The Stilled Disciple"

**Status:** Generated (NPE Step 2)
**Genre:** Cozy Mystery / Fantasy (Cozy Module v1.2)
**Premise:** When a beloved healer is turned to stone, a dragon librarian must uncover the truth behind a "feral" Gorgon rumor before a desperate mother makes a choice that will silence the town forever.

---

## 1. THE CORE MYSTERY
**The Event:** Brother Demos is found petrified in the Temple Archives.
**The Truth:** Sister Photine (Head Healer) petrified him to prevent him from reporting her for using forbidden magic to save her secret daughter, Melaina.
**The Twist:** Demos was not attacking; he was trying to "save" Photine from her own exhaustion. The "Monster" is maternal love in a scarcity trap.

## 3. THE SUSPECT FLOWER (The Gorgon Enclave)
*The intersection of the suspect pool is the **Cistern District Enclave**. All suspects are Gorgons who embody the theme of "The Mask vs. The Gaze."*

### Suspect A: Brother Demos (The Victim)
*   **Role:** The Bureaucrat. Found petrified.
*   **Relation:** Photine's colleague. Discovered her secret mana drain.

### Suspect B: Kyra Kallistos (The False Face)
*   **Archetype:** The Debutante.
*   **Theme:** **Passing.**
*   **Secret:** She is a Gorgon who "passes" as human. Demos threatened to out her.
*   **Alibi:** "Shopping" (Actually buying blackmail material).

### Suspect C: Mother Ligeia (The Shield)
*   **Archetype:** The Gatekeeper.
*   **Theme:** **Isolation.**
*   **Secret:** She runs the Enclave as a fortress. She hates Demos for his "Safety Inspections."
*   **Motive:** To protect the sanctuary from Demos's condemnation order.

### Suspect D: Melaina (The False Target)
*   **Archetype:** The Patient.
*   **Theme:** **The Gaze.**
*   **Role:** Photine's secret daughter. Suffering from "Medusa's Weep."
*   **Twist:** Found petrified at the Midpoint (Chapter 10).

### Suspect E: Xanthe (The Glare)
*   **Archetype:** The Radical.
*   **Theme:** **Defiance.**
*   **Role:** Explicitly militant.
*   **Motive:** Revenge. Demos refused Triage treatment to her brother last month.

### Suspect F: Myron (The Mirror)
*   **Archetype:** The Artist.
*   **Theme:** **Sublimation.**
*   **Role:** A sculptor who makes "life-like" statues.
*   **Motive:** Artistic Jealousy. Demos called his work "Abomination."

## 4. WORLD & SETTING
**Solterra Bay:** A sun-drenched coastal town where magic is "Cozy" (convenient, not combat).
**The Scarcity Field:** There is a finite amount of Mana/Medicine available. This drives the tragedy.
**The Enclave:** A damp, echoing cistern district where Gorgons live to avoid accidental eye contact.

## 5. STAKES & PROMISE
*   **Emotional:** Will Cornelius accept that "Monsters" are made, not born?
*   **External:** If Demos isn't un-frozen by Sunset on Day 3, he dies.
*   **The Promise:** Order will be restored. Demos will live. The community will learn to support the Enclave better.
