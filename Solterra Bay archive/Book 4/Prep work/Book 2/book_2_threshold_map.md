# STRUCTURAL STORY PLAN
**Project:** Solterra Bay - Book 2: "The Stilled Disciple"

## STRUCTURE OVERVIEW
*   **Type:** 4-Part "Cozy Ticking Clock" Mystery.
*   **Justification:** The 72-hour limit dictates a compressed, high-pressure timeline. The "Cozy" genre requires a focus on relationships and community healing alongside the puzzle.
*   **Chapter Count:** 20 Chapters (Standard Solterra Bay Grid).

## NARRATIVE PHASES

### Phase 1: The Stone Case (Ch 1-5)
*   **Function:** Destabilization. The "Safe" temple is violated.
*   **Dominant Axis:** `Visibility` (The hidden threat becomes visible).
*   **Entropy:** Rising (0.2 -> 0.4).

### Phase 2: The Silence (Ch 6-10)
*   **Function:** Escalation/Confusion. The "Killer" strikes again (Melaina).
*   **Dominant Axis:** `Trust` (Everyone suspects everyone).
*   **Entropy:** High (0.4 -> 0.7).

### Phase 3: The Desperate Hours (Ch 11-15)
*   **Function:** Investigation/Reveal. The false leads crumble.
*   **Dominant Axis:** `Justice` (Vigilante Mob vs. Law).
*   **Entropy:** Peak Chaos (0.7 -> 0.9).

### Phase 4: The Race (Ch 16-20)
*   **Function:** Synthesis/Rescue.
*   **Dominant Axis:** `Mercy` (Saving Demos > Catching Photine).
*   **Entropy:** Collapsing to Order (0.9 -> 0.1).

## GENRE MODULE TOPOLOGY (Attractors)
*   **Attractor A (The Body):** Ch 2 (Demos found).
*   **Attractor B (The Twist):** Ch 10 (Melaina found).
*   **Attractor C (The False Accusation):** Ch 15 (Mob targets the Enclave).
*   **Attractor D (The Reveal):** Ch 19 (The Docks).
