
**act_purpose:** Deconstruct the "Simple Theory" (Petros is the killer). Cornelius investigates the suspects, gathers clues that seem to point one way, hits a bureaucratic wall, breaks the rules to get "proof," and suffers a devastating public reversal when his theory is disproven by an ironclad alibi.

**major_act_reveals:**
*   **Sophia Doukas (Suspect B)** hides her stress with physical items (scarf) and has a tangible link to Photine (Blue Thread).
*   **Captain Mago (Suspect C)** confirms Elias was in debt and promised "Kallistos Gold" to pay it off, establishing Elias as the active thief.
*   **Petros Kallistos (Suspect D)** is **INNOCENT**. He has an ironclad alibi (in jail during the murder) and reveals Elias tricked him into writing the vault code.
*   The "Kallistos Letters" were the primary target, and Elias likely had a partner or a boss (The "Teacher"?).
*   Cornelius's arrogance leads him to publicly accuse the wrong man, turning the community against him.
