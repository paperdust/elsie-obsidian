
**act_purpose:** Establish Cornelius's "Silence" (equilibrium), introduce the closed circle of Solterra Bay, and shatter that peace with the murder of Elias and the theft of the Kallistos Letters, forcing Cornelius to abandon his passive role and become a detective.

**major_act_reveals:**
*   Elias of Tyre was murdered in the Kallistos Vault, and the "Kallistos Letters" were stolen.
*   The Library Vault was breached using magic (Ozone smell) and mechanics (Lapis/Oil residue), implying a sophisticated thief.
*   The "Flower" of suspects is established: Sophia (The Rival), Mago (The Debtor), Petros (The False Target), Photine (The Gossip), Eudora (The Hirer).
*   The Watch (Theo Melas) has ruled the death natural/accidental and closed the vault, effectively locking Cornelius out of his own investigation.
*   Cornelius has decided to investigate "off the books" with Zeph, marking the transition from Librarian to Sleuth.

### tentpole_events

**tentpole_event_1:** **The Inciting Incident (Ch 2):** Discovery of the body and the empty shelf. The violation of the sanctuary.

**tentpole_event_2:** **The Complication (Ch 3):** Captain Theo Melas takes control, dismisses Cornelius's evidence, and shuts down the investigation.

**tentpole_event_3:** **The Threshold (Ch 5):** Cornelius rejects the official verdict and creates his own "Case Board," formally committing to the mystery.

### chronological_event_timeline
