### main_character_1

#### basic_information

**full_name:** Cornelius Drake (The Library Dragon)

**nicknames_terms_of_endearment:** "Boss" (by Zeph), "Lizard" (derogatory), "Cute" (by unsuspecting humans).

**age:** 127 (Adult).

**profession_or_role:** Archivist / Self-Proclaimed Forensic Detective.

**nationality:** Draconic (Solterra Bay Resident).

**current_residence:** The Solterra Bay Library (specifically the rafters or his hoard).

**personality_traits:** Haughty, fastenious, protective, logically rigid, socially awkward but secretly deeply caring.

**physical_description:** A large house-cat sized dragon (12-14 lbs). Scales of burnished bronze with copper undertones. Eyes like liquid gold behind custom spectacles. Wingspan approx 3 feet.

**notable_interests:** Book preservation, fresh mint, live crickets, order/categorization.

**backstory_essentials:** Bonded to the library as an egg when the collection reached critical mass. Hatched by Imelda. Views the library as his "Hoard."

#### motivation_and_wounds

**primary_motivation:** To protect and organize his Hoard (the library and its staff).

**core_wound:** The fear that he is small and powerless compared to "True Dragons" (Capital City dragons are bus-sized).

**central_flaw:** Arrogance masking insecurity. He tries to control everything because he fears chaos.

**internal_conflict:** Mentorship vs. Control. He wants Zeph/the camp to succeed, but terrified of the mess it creates.

**internal_goal:** To be respected as a formidable guardian despite his size.

**external_personal_goal:** Secure the library grant to ensure financial stability.

**external_conflict_career_or_life:** The Campers are messy, loud, and stickyâ€”everything he hates.

**external_conflict_public_or_family:** The Town Council (and Little Kyria's report) threatening to shut him down.

### character_story_arc

**starting_state:** Stressed, rigid, trying to micro-manage the camp into "Dignified Silence."

**growth_change_process:** Forced to rely on "Chaos" (Zeph, the children) to solve the mystery. Learns that stories/magic cannot be strictly controlled, only guided.

**ending_state:** Accepts "Messy Survival" as a victory. Acknowledges Zeph as a peer.

### voice_and_inner_world

**voice_description:** Precise, slightly antiquated, melodramatic but dignified.

**language_style:** High vocabulary. Never uses contractions in internal monologue. Uses forensic/academic terms for emotional situations.

**dialogue_style:** Blustering, lectures, often interrupted by reality. Narrates human behavior like a nature documentary.

**emotional_depth:** Deeply sentimental but hides it behind "Logic" and "Duty."

**internal_voice_sample:** *The young human arrived precisely when I expected: late. He brought with him the damp brow of anxiety and... crumbs of cheese upon his tunic. Why must humans always be so messy? It is a biological inefficiency I shall never comprehend.*

### main_character_2

#### basic_information

**full_name:** Imelda Kassiani

**nicknames_terms_of_endearment:** "The Anchor."

**age:** 187 (Cyprian Elf - appears late 60s).

**profession_or_role:** Head Librarian.

**nationality:** Cyprian Elf (Solterra Bay Native).

**current_residence:** Apartment above the Library.

**personality_traits:** Calm, unshakeable, observant, patient, authoritative (soft power).

**physical_description:** Silver-white hair in intricate braids with copper beads. Nut-brown skin heavily lined. Hands stained with ink/metal dust.

**notable_interests:** Mending (books and people), tea, managing Cornelius.

**backstory_essentials:** Hatched Cornelius. She is the only one who remembers the grandparents of the current council.

#### motivation_and_wounds

**primary_motivation:** To maintain the library as a sanctuary for the town.

**core_wound:** The burden of outliving her human friends (Elven longevity).

**central_flaw:** Can be too protective of Cornelius, shielding him from reality.

**internal_conflict:** Balancing the budget vs. the mission of the library.

**internal_goal:** To ensure the library survives after she is gone (Legacy).

**external_personal_goal:** Keep the Camp from exploding.

**external_conflict_career_or_life:** Managing the egos of the Town Council.

**external_conflict_public_or_family:** None major, she is the matriarch.

### character_story_arc

**starting_state:** Bemused by Cornelius's panic, holding the fort.

**growth_change_process:** Supports the "cover-up" not out of protocol, but out of love for the children.

**ending_state:** Validated in her trust of the community.

### voice_and_inner_world

**voice_description:** Warm, measured, amused. The voice of someone who has seen it all.

**language_style:** Gentle, polite, but with a steel core.

**dialogue_style:** Uses questions to guide people. "Shall we assume...?"

**emotional_depth:** Shows emotion through action (making tea, mending). Very meaningful silence.

**internal_voice_sample:** *Cornelius is vibrating again. I shall have to fetch the mint. He does worry so about the rules, as if rules were made of stone and not suggestions written on very pliable parchment.*

### main_character_3

**nicknames_terms_of_endearment:** "The Intern," "Boy."

**age:** 16.

**profession_or_role:** Library Intern / Camp Counselor.

**nationality:** Human (Lamassu-touched).

**current_residence:** Solterra Bay (Parents' house).

**personality_traits:** Reluctant, secretly competent, emphatic, awkward, brave.

**physical_description:** Lanky, growth-spurt awkwardness. Dark skin, unruly black hair with a metallic bronze sheen.

**notable_interests:** Surving the internship, "Coolness" (fails at it), helping kids.

**backstory_essentials:** Descended from ancient guardians (Lamassu) but doesn't know it yet. Doing this for school credit.

#### motivation_and_wounds

**primary_motivation:** To prove he isn't useless.

**core_wound:** Feeling like an impostor/fraud.

**central_flaw:** Hides his caring behind sarcasm.

**internal_conflict:** Wants to help but afraid of failing Cornelius's high standards.

**internal_goal:** To be recognized as a "Guardian."

**external_personal_goal:** Get a good grade on his internship report.

**external_conflict_career_or_life:** Managing a herd of magical children who don't listen.

**external_conflict_public_or_family:** His family expects him to do well.

### character_story_arc

**starting_state:** Overwhelmed, trying to be "cool," afraid of Cornelius.

**growth_change_process:** Steps up when the adults crack. Becomes the "Ringmaster" of the animals.

**ending_state:** Confident, accepted by Cornelius as a partner.

### voice_and_inner_world

**voice_description:** Sarcastic, mumbled, teenage.

**language_style:** Slang, short sentences, defensive.

**dialogue_style:** "Whatever," "I guess." Deflects sincerity with humor.

**emotional_depth:** High empathy, but embarrassing for him to show it.

**internal_voice_sample:** *You want me to climb into the vent? You realize I don't have wings, right? Or insurance? This place is a death trap with books.*

### main_character_4

#### basic_information

**full_name:** Pippa Miller

**nicknames_terms_of_endearment:** "Pip," "The Decorator."

**age:** 9.

**profession_or_role:** Camper / Antagonist (Unintentional).

**nationality:** Human (Nymph/Dryad Heritage).

**current_residence:** Solterra Bay.

**personality_traits:** Imaginative, sweet, intense, lonely, powerful.

**physical_description:** Small, messy pigtails, always covered in glitter/flour. Big eyes.

**notable_interests:** Fairy tales (Grimm style), decorating, "Making things pretty."

**backstory_essentials:** Her grandmother was a Dryad from the deep woods. Her parents suppressed the magic, causing it to flare up.

#### motivation_and_wounds

**primary_motivation:** To be "Special" and make the world like her stories.

**core_wound:** Being told her imagination isn't real/is "superstition."

**central_flaw:** Unchecked reality warping.

**internal_conflict:** Wants to help but hurts people by accident.

**internal_goal:** To find a place where her magic is accepted.

**external_personal_goal:** Win the Bake-Off decoration prize.

**external_conflict_career_or_life:** Her parents don't understand her.

**external_conflict_public_or_family:** She turns her brother into a pigeon.

### character_story_arc

**starting_state:** Isolated, reading in corners, leaking magic.

**growth_change_process:** Confronted with the harm she caused. Accepted by her mother.

**ending_state:** Apprenticed, learning to control her gift.

### voice_and_inner_world

**voice_description:** Childlike, earnest, whispery.

**language_style:** Simple, declarative. Quotes stories often.

**dialogue_style:** "Once upon a time..." "It says here..."

**emotional_depth:** Pure, raw emotion. Zero filter.

**internal_voice_sample:** *It needs to be sparkles. If it sparkles, it's magic. And if it's magic, then the Prince will come.*
