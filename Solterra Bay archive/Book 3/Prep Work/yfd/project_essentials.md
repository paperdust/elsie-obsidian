**expanded_premise:** Cornelius Drake wants nothing more than to organize his library in peace. But Solterra Bay is a town of feuding Tile Families, gossip, and sticky pastries. When the famous author Fabrizio Lucchesi drops dead at the Mayor's Gala, Cornelius is the only one with the "Forensic Vision" to see the clues.
Partnered with Zeph, an intern whose migraines are actually a magical danger-sense, Cornelius must navigate a suspect list that includes Sirens, Gorgons, and Elves. He must prove that the "Obvious Suspect" (a Sphinx-kin ghostwriter) is innocent, catch the true killer, and learn that sometimes, trusting others is the only logical choice.

### unique_selling_points

**usp_1:** **The Protagonist:** A Dragon who is *not* a shape-shifter, *not* a giant monster, but a cat-sized intellectual who is physically vulnerable but mentally superior.

**usp_2:** **The Setting:** "Mediterranean Solarpunk." A fantasy world without "Dark Lords" or "War." The stakes are local, personal, and communal.

### themes

**primary_theme:** **Order vs. Community:** The tension between being "Right" (Logic/Cornelius) and being "Good" (Connection/Zeph).

**secondary_theme_1:** **The Value of the Outsider:** Cornelius and Valerian are "Other," but their unique perspectives solve the crime.

**secondary_theme_2:** **Legacy:** Zeph accepting his heritage; The Tile Families fighting over theirs.
