
**act_purpose:** Establish the "Golden Hour" sensory aesthetic of Solterra Bay and Cornelius's deep desire for order and solitude. Introduce the "Closed Circle" of the Gala as a pressure cooker of social obligation. End with the murder of Fabrizio Lucchesi and Cornelius's decision to investigate to save his book and his intern.

**major_act_reveals:**
*   Fabrizio Lucchesi is dead (poisoned).
*   The poison is "Judgement Salt" (kills only the guilty).
*   The Gala is locked down; no one leaves.
*   Cornelius has forensic "Dragon Vision".
*   Zeph has mysterious "migraines" that act as danger sense.

### tentpole_events

**tentpole_event_1:** **The Gala Opening.** Cornelius arrives at City Hall, establishing his disdain for chaos and his obsession with getting his book signed. Zeph is introduced as the anxious intern dating the heiress.

**tentpole_event_2:** **The Body Drop (Inciting Incident).** During the keynote toast, Fabrizio Lucchesi drinks and immediately dies, shattering the order Cornelius prizes.

**tentpole_event_3:** **The Lockdown.** Captain Theo bars the doors, trapping the killer and the dragon inside. Cornelius realizes he must solve the crime to escape and get his book signed.
