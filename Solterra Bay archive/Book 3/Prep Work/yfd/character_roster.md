### main_character_1

#### basic_information

**full_name:** Cornelius Drake
**nicknames_terms_of_endearment:** The Library Dragon, Lizard (by Zeph, derogatory), "Oh, him" (by Town Watch).
**age:** 127 (Adult, roughly 30s-40s in draconic maturity).
**profession_or_role:** The Library Dragon / Reluctant Detective.
**nationality:** Solterra Bay Native (Manifested in the Library).
**current_residence:** The Solterra Bay Library (specifically, atop the highest bookshelf or in his hoard).
**personality_traits:** Haughty, Order-Obsessed, Secretly Protective, Fastidious, Melodramatic.
**physical_description:** Large house cat size (12-14 lbs). Scales of burnished bronze with copper undertones. Eyes like liquid gold behind custom spectacles. Wingspan approx 3 feet.
**notable_interests:** Hoarding books, eating fresh mint & live crickets, correcting grammar, criticizing human inefficiency.
**backstory_essentials:** He did not hatch from an egg; he manifested from the "Critical Mass of Knowledge" in the library. He believes he is the guardian of civilization (i.e., the books).

#### motivation_and_wounds

**primary_motivation:** To maintain Order and protect his Hoard (The Library).
**core_wound:** Fear that he is merely a "pet" or a "freak" rather than a true Dragon of Legend. (Compensates with extreme dignity).
**central_flaw:** Arrogance. He dismisses emotions/people as "messy data."
**internal_conflict:** Desire for solitude vs. subconscious need for his "Hoard of People" (Imelda, Zeph).
**internal_goal:** To be recognized as an Authority/Intellectual Superior.
**external_personal_goal:** To get through the Gala without social interaction.
**external_conflict_career_or_life:** Captain Theo interfering with his investigations; Zeph organizing shelves "wrong."
**external_conflict_public_or_family:** The Town Council failing to fund the humidity controls.

### character_story_arc

**starting_state:** Detached observer. Wants the murder solved quickly so he can go home.
**growth_change_process:** Forced to rely on Zeph's "illogical" intuition and Imelda's community connections. Learns that justice is messier than law.
**ending_state:** Accepts his role as a Protector of the *community*, not just the books.

### voice_and_inner_world

**voice_description:** Precise, slightly antiquated, melodramatic. Low, resonant thrum.
**language_style:** High diction. Never uses contractions in monologue. Uses scientific/taxonomic terms for mundane things.
**dialogue_style:** Sharp, correcting, often condescending but witty.
**emotional_depth:** Profound but deeply repressed. He feels affection as "concern for asset maintenance."
**internal_voice_sample:** *The young human arrived precisely when I expected: late. He brought with him the damp brow of anxiety and... crumbs of cheese upon his tunic. Why must humans always be so messy? It is a statistical improbability that they have survived as a species given their lack of self-preservation instincts.*

### main_character_2

#### basic_information

**full_name:** Zephyr "Zeph" Taraki
**nicknames_terms_of_endearment:** Zeph, The Intern, Boy (by Cornelius).
**age:** 16
**profession_or_role:** Library Intern / The "Legs" of the investigation.
**nationality:** Solterran (mixed heritage).
**current_residence:** Parents' house (Lower District).
**personality_traits:** Sarcastic, Practical, Intuitively Organized, Anxious, Loyal.
**physical_description:** Lanky, awkward growth phase. Dark skin. Unruly black hair that gleams with an unnatural *bronze* sheen in sunlight.
**notable_interests:** Surviving his internship, impressing Sophia Doukas, organizing chaos.
**backstory_essentials:** Taken on as an intern for school credit. Unaware he is "Lamassu-touched" (Guardian Spirit ancestry), which manifests as migraines near danger.

#### motivation_and_wounds

**primary_motivation:** To prove he isn't a screw-up; to be seen as competent (especially by Sophia).
**core_wound:** Believes his "danger sense" headaches are just stress/weakness.
**central_flaw:** Insecurity. He defaults to sarcasm to hide fear.
**internal_conflict:** Wanting to belong (be normal) vs. being fundamentally "Other" (Lamassu heritage).
**internal_goal:** To understand his own worth.
**external_personal_goal:** To survive the Gala date with Sophia without embarrassing himself.
**external_conflict_career_or_life:** Managing Cornelius's impossible demands.
**external_conflict_public_or_family:** The pressure of dating the Heiress of House Doukas.

### character_story_arc

**starting_state:** Overwhelmed intern trying to act cool.
**growth_change_process:** Discovers his headaches are a radar for danger. Learns to trust his gut over Cornelius's logic.
**ending_state:** Confident partner. Accepts his "Legacy" (vaguely).

### voice_and_inner_world

**voice_description:** Cracking slightly, rapid-fire when nervous, deadpan when done.
**language_style:** Teenage casual, but intelligent.
**dialogue_style:** Deflective sarcasm. "Yeah, sure, whatever."
**emotional_depth:** High anxiety masking deep loyalty.
**internal_voice_sample:** *Great. The lizard wants me to climb the vent. Because apparently, 'intern' is just High Draconic for 'expendable trap-disarmer.' I swear, if I singe my eyebrows again, I'm filing a grievance with the Guild. Assuming I survive the fall.*

### main_character_3

#### basic_information

**full_name:** Captain Theodoros "Theo" Melas
**nicknames_terms_of_endearment:** Theo, Cap, "The Obstacle."
**age:** 44
**profession_or_role:** Captain of the Town Watch.
**nationality:** Solterran.
**current_residence:** Watch Barracks / Small apartment above the jail.
**personality_traits:** Exhausted, Rule-Bound, Honorable, Cynical, Dutiful.
**physical_description:** Weathered, tired eyes, permanent stubble. Uniform clean but worn.
**notable_interests:** Law, Order, Sleep (rare), strong coffee.
**backstory_essentials:** A good man in a hard job with no budget. Dealing with magical feuds and tile mafias has aged him.

#### motivation_and_wounds

**primary_motivation:** To keep the peace in Solterra Bay.
**core_wound:** The belief that "Justice" and "The Law" are drifting apart, and he can't fix it.
**central_flaw:** Inflexibility. He trusts procedure over truth.
**internal_conflict:** Duty vs. Instinct. He knows Cornelius is often right, but hates his methods.
**internal_goal:** To rest. (Subconsciously: To share the burden).
**external_personal_goal:** Close the Lucchesi case before the Mayor fires him.
**external_conflict_career_or_life:** The Mayor's political pressure; the Factions' interference.

### character_story_arc

**starting_state:** Blocking Cornelius to follow protocol.
**growth_change_process:** Realizes protocol can't solve a magical mystery.
**ending_state:** Allies with Cornelius. Respects the "Irregular" approach.

### voice_and_inner_world

**voice_description:** Gravelly, tired, flat.
**language_style:** Police procedural, short sentences.
**dialogue_style:** "Just the facts." No patience for whimsy.
**emotional_depth:** Buried under layers of exhaustion.
**internal_voice_sample:** *I don't get paid enough for dragons. I barely get paid enough for drunks. If he sets fire to my crime scene one more time, I'm arresting him for public nuisance. Who cares if the cell is too big? I'll put him in a birdcage.*
