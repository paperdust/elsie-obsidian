# Chapter 10: The Second Poisoning (MIDPOINT)

The Rotunda. The guests are gathered in a circle around the podium. The dome echoes Cornelius's accusation.
Valerian stands on the lower steps.
Cornelius looms above him.
When Valerian drinks, the sound of his collapse reverberates against the marble walls.
The chaos is total. The "Civic Order" represented by the building is completely inverted.
