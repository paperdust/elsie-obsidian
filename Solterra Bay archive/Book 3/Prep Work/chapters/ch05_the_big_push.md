# Chapter 5: The Big Push

Cornelius drags Zeph into the antechamber of the Mayor's Office.
"We are going to interview them," Cornelius says.
"Here? In the Mayor's office?"
"It's the only room with decent chairs," Cornelius says.
They look through the glass doors at the suspects gathered in the reception area.
Valerian, Pavlos, Chloe, Imelda, Jasper, Elara.
Against the backdrop of City Hall's oil paintings, they look like a tableau of guilt.
