Cornelius is skeptical, noting her disheveled state and muddy hem. He smells "Synthetic Musk" on her but checks the window. He sees Brother Demos sprinting away down the alley, clutching a leather-bound book that perfectly matches the missing volume *and* the journal's description. The logic snaps into place: Demos is the thief. Cornelius accepts the case, motivated by the intellectual puzzle and the desire to punish a book thief.
